VIBAL Studios

Marketing Collateral Development

UNLEASHING CREATIVITY ON PAPER FOR VIBAL STUDIOS​

The Ask

“With Vibal Studios’ launch date set for November 2024, we need a B2B sales presentation that can be used by our stakeholders, from management to sales agents, to acquire new business and partnerships.”

The Solution​

Drawing on VIBAL Studio’s established brand CI manual, STRE crafted a 30-page sales presentation utilized by VIBAL stakeholders to pursue new business opportunities in anticipation of the launch of Bangkok's most extensive creative ecosystem to date. Assembling this presentation required coordinated efforts from multiple creative teams and partners, including tasks such as structuring and refining the deck's flow, planning visual direction, conducting copywriting and sales note preparation, and creating one-of-a-kind content and artwork. ​
